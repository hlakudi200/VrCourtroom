Enter the Virtual Courtroom

Put on your VR headset and step into a lifelike courtroom environment. Explore the space, familiarize yourself with the courtroom layout, and interact with various elements.

Investigate and Prepare

Review case files, gather evidence, and interview virtual clients and witnesses. Use your legal knowledge to analyze the facts and build a strong case strategy.

Present Your Case

Take on the role of a lawyer and present your arguments before a virtual judge and jury. Engage in realistic court proceedings, including cross-examinations and objections, to advocate for your client.

Receive Feedback and Refine

After the trial, receive detailed feedback on your performance. Reflect on your strategy, learn from your mistakes, and replay scenarios to improve your legal skills.
